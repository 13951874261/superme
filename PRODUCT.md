## Users
Professional learners, financial executives, and business negotiators looking to acquire advanced business English through high-impact 외제/foreign materials, business columns, financial reports, and videos.

## Product Purpose
B·AI High-End Digital Sandtable is a vocabulary-mining and language-practice workspace that feeds real-world articles, URLs, and video transcribes into Dify-powered AI knowledge extraction and saves key terms to an Ebbinghaus memory review book.

## Brand Personality
Professional, authoritative, focused, and premium.

## Anti-references
- Toy-like, gamified interfaces (e.g., Duolingo's pastel colors and cartoon characters).
- Low-contrast, hard-to-read text columns.
- Cluttered, disorganized dashboards with excessive widgets.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity Over Clutter**: Restrained dark-mode accents and structured card views that guide the eyes through inputs and outcomes.
- **Immediate Context**: Users should see what they are about to feed the AI before running workflows.
- **Frictionless Feedback**: Status and progress must be visible, with granular logs available for deep inspection.
