# Business Negotiation: Product Pricing and Terms

## Background
Acme Corp and Beta Distributing are negotiating the procurement of 10,000 smart widgets.

## Negotiation Transcript
**Acme (Seller):** We are offering a unit price of $12.50 for our premium widgets, with a standard warranty of 1 year.
**Beta (Buyer):** That is slightly above our budget of $10.00. We can agree to $11.00 if we increase the order volume to 12,000 units.
**Acme:** If you order 12,000 units, the best we can offer is $11.50 per unit. However, we can extend the warranty to 18 months.
**Beta:** That sounds reasonable. What about the shipping costs?
**Acme:** Shipping is FOB origin, but for this volume, we can share 50% of the logistics costs.

## Vocabulary List
- widget (noun): a small gadget or mechanical device.
- procurement (noun): the action of obtaining or procuring something.
- premium (adjective): of high quality and more expensive.
- logistics (noun): the detailed coordination of a complex operation involving many people, facilities, or supplies.
